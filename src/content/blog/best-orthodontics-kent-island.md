## Why is Orthodontics Important?

Orthodontics deals with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of dental irregularities. Without treatment, crooked, missing teeth, or bite problems may lead to cumulative complications years later, such as inability to keep teeth clean, speech distortions, and teeth grinding, making some teeth wear out faster than others.

A healthy and straight smile boosts self-esteem. Both children and adults can get help through orthodontic care, and due to the modern technology available these days, it is more comfortable and discreet to get orthodontic care.
