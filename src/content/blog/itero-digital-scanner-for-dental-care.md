## How Does the iTero Digital Dental Scanner Work?

Using the iTero digital dental scanner is simple and patient-friendly. Here's how the system works.

- **Scanning:** During scanning, a dentist captures thousands of images per second by gently moving the handheld wand around the patient's mouth.
- **3D Imaging:** To create a comprehensive 3D model of your teeth and gums, dentists compile these images.
- **Analysis:** The comprehensive 3D model of your teeth and gums is used to assess dental issues, plan customized treatments, and design custom appliances like Invisalign aligners.
- **Immediate Feedback:** The iTero digital dental scanner allows patients to view their results on the screen. This approach provides transparency and a better understanding of your dental health.
