### How Does Invisalign for Adults Work?

In Invisalign treatment for adults, there is a series of custom-made clear aligners that gradually shift your teeth into the desired position. Also, this treatment is different from traditional metal braces because these aligners allow for better oral hygiene, are easily removable, and, most importantly, don't restrict you from eating your desired foods.

**Initial Consultation—The** first step is the initial consultation, where the orthodontist evaluates your teeth and discusses your smile goals.

**Custom Aligners**—In the second step, they use 3D imaging technology so that your aligners get crafted to fit perfectly.

**Gradual Progress**—The process is gradual as you switch to a new set of aligners every one to two weeks to ensure continuous movement.

**Final Results**—After completing the Invisalign treatment plan, you'll achieve a beautifully aligned smile.

### The Benefits of Invisalign for Adults

**Invisalign braces for adults** have become a preferred choice because they offer various benefits. Mostly, if you're a busy professional, parent, or anyone who is looking for a simple method to correct your teeth, you will prefer this method. Let's explore its benefits—

1. **Discreet Appearance**

One of the most important benefits of Invisalign is its discreet appearance. No one can notice you're undergoing this treatment due to its clear and virtually invisible aligners.

2. **No Dietary Restrictions**

There is no kind of dietary restriction in Invisalign, but in metal braces, there is a restriction on eating certain types of foods. During your Invisalign treatment, you can remove them and enjoy your favorite food.

3. **Easy Oral Care**

During metal braces treatment, you can't remove aligners for a certain time period, which leads to gum disease and risk of cavities. But with Invisalign aligners, you can remove your aligners, brush and floss your teeth, which helps maintain good oral hygiene.

4. **Comfortable & Custom Fit**

With Invisalign aligners, you will feel comfortable without irritation, as these aligners are custom-designed to fit your teeth perfectly.

5. **Boost in Confidence**

Invisalign aligners result in a perfect and confident smile that enhances your self-esteem, making your social interactions more enjoyable.

### Whether to choose Invisalign or traditional braces

Many adults wonder whether to choose **braces or Invisalign for adults** while considering orthodontic treatment. Let's explore both of them.

**Aesthetic Appeal**—You can maintain your stylish look and appearance even while having Invisalign aligners but not with metal braces. Invisalign aligners provide virtually invisible aligners that are not visible to anyone.

**Comfort & Convenience**—Invisalign aligners have smooth edges that don't provide any kind of irritation, but metal braces cause irritation with their edges.

**Removable**—You can enjoy eating and drinking with Invisalign aligners. Also, you can clean your teeth without any restrictions that offer oral hygiene. But metal [braces](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dental_braces) have restrictions and don't provide oral hygiene.

### What to Expect During Invisalign Treatment for Adults

There are a few things that you have to expect during your Invisalign treatment, as you have to wear them 20-22 hours per day.

- **Initial Discomfort:** During your initial phase of Invisalign aligners, you can feel some discomfort as it subsides quickly. When you switch to a new set of aligners, some pressure is normal.

- **Regular Check-Ups:** You need to make regular check-ups to ensure the treatment is progressing correctly.

- **Post-Treatment Retainers:** Wearing a retainer will help you maintain your new smile after completing the Invisalign process.
