## What's the Difference Between a Dentist and an Orthodontist?

The most frequently asked questions among adults are the ones that deal with a **dentist vs orthodontist**. Dentists provide general dental care, including cleanings, fillings, and preventive therapy. Orthodontists, on the other hand, also get extra special education aimed at fixing misaligned teeth and jaws.

An orthodontist is the best professional to visit in case you need to straighten your teeth or correct some of the problems associated with your bite. Although your dentist could suggest orthodontic treatment, only a qualified orthodontist is capable of efficiently planning and overseeing therapy.
