## New Teeth Straightening Solutions for Adults

At **Benkovich Orthodontics**, we remain abreast of the latest orthodontic technology in order to provide our patients with the highest level of care.

Some of the most recent trends in straightening adult teeth are outlined below:

### 1. Clear Aligners

Clear aligners have revolutionized orthodontic treatment for adults. The nearly invisible trays are made to fit snugly over your teeth and move them gradually into place. As a top [**Annapolis orthodontist MD**](/), we offer clear aligner options that bring the following advantages:

- **Unobtrusive Appearance:** Clear aligners are virtually invisible, so they are an ideal solution for adults worried about the looks of conventional braces.
- **Removability:** You can remove the aligners during meals, beverages, and brushing your teeth for more convenience and ease of maintenance.
- **Comfort:** Without metal wires or brackets, clear aligners lower the risk of mouth irritation.

Our **Annapolis orthodontist** staff at **Benkovich Orthodontics** will consult with you to establish whether or not clear aligners are suitable for your orthodontic needs.

### 2. Wilckodontics

For those seeking accelerated orthodontic treatment, Wilckodontics offers a groundbreaking solution. This technique combines traditional orthodontic methods with a minor surgical procedure to expedite tooth movement. As an experienced **orthodontist Annapolis,** Dr. Benkovich is skilled in performing Wilckodontics, which can significantly reduce treatment time compared to conventional braces.

### 3. iTero Digital Impression System

Those days of awkward, messy dental impressions are a thing of the past. At [**Benkovich Orthodontics**](/), we use the iTero Digital Impression System to produce accurate 3D images of your gums and teeth. This technology improves the accuracy of treatment planning and gives you the ability to see your treatment result before treatment begins. Our experienced **Annapolis orthodontist** strives to include leading-edge technology in every treatment.
