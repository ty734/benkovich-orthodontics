### Adult Treatment Options

The needs of each patient are different, and orthodontic care is not a one-size-fits-all solution. With Adult Orthodontics in Kent Island, there are a number of different treatment options available to adults to fit their lives and expectations.

**Clear Aligners** – Adults will prefer using clear aligners because they directly achieve teeth straightening without the use of metal braces. The removable trays gently shift teeth into place over time.

**Ceramic Braces** – Ceramic braces can be used when your problem requires more extended correction and you want a discreet appearance, as they match the color of your natural teeth and provide you with a spectacular outcome.

**Removable Appliances** – Adults benefit from being able to remove the device at work or during important occasions. Removable orthodontic treatment in Kent Island provides a convenient solution that fits easily into daily routines.
