## Introduction to the Metal Braces Basics

Metal braces are the orthodontic norm that has prevailed for decades. These are made of strong stainless steel and use small brackets and wires to slowly move the teeth into the right position. They have a long duration of efficacy and are easy to use, especially with more youthful patients.

The modern metal braces are more slender and comfortable when you visit a trusted [**braces doctor in Kent Island.**](/about/meet-the-team-dental/) The smaller size of the bracket and the smoother finish make it less obtrusive and still very efficient.

### The Benefits of Clear Braces

However, it is possible to choose a less noticeable alternative, clear braces. They use ceramic brackets that match the natural color of your teeth, making them much less noticeable. It is an immense advantage for adult or adolescent patients who may be self-conscious about their looks when they are undergoing orthodontic work.

Clear braces may offer the same stable outcomes as metal braces with the added benefit of aesthetic appeal. You can investigate [**Kent Island oral braces**](/braces-info/about-orthodontics/) through clear braces as well. Even though they sometimes take a bit of extra care in order to avoid staining, most patients find that such a trade-off is worth it in terms of the confidence they enjoy during the process.

### About the Differences between Clear Braces vs Metal in Kent Island, MD

At **Clear Braces vs Metal in Kent Island, MD,** some factors come into play:

- Looks- People will notice clear braces a lot less than they will notice metal braces.
- Durability: Metal braces are much stronger and can resist more damage, whereas the brackets of the clear braces (ceramic) can be a bit more breakable.
- Comfort – Patients usually find clear braces to be very comfortable.
- Care – Braces made of ceramic will have to be cleaned thoroughly in order to prevent staining, whereas metal ones are less demanding.
