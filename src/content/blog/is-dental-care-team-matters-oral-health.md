When considering good oral health, most individuals consider a single individual: the dentist. However, there is much more to that healthy smile than meets the eye, and that is an entire [**dental care team**](/about/meet-the-team-dental/) working in unison to ensure that you receive the best of care. Everybody is important: a receptionist who greets you, a hygienist who cleans your teeth, and a dentist who fixes any problems.

This is why having a good dental care team will go a long way in your overall health status and dental experience.

## You Get Well-Rounded Care Completely

Dental health is not simply a single issue or a single procedure. It is prevention, education, early diagnosis, and long-term care. A powerful dental care team encompasses all those areas. For example:

- The hygienist aids in the prevention of issues by cleaning your teeth and instructing you on the proper brushing and flossing.
- The dental assistant ensures that treatments are easily performed and that you are comfortable.
- The front office staff makes sure that your visits are on time, and assists with insurance as well as answering any questions.
- Diagnosis and complicated treatment are in the hands of the dentist.

The dental care team together helps to ensure that no detail is overlooked and that all aspects of your care are addressed.

## You Develop Trust and Become More Comfortable

The very first thought of a dentist may be stressful, more so if you have had a poor experience previously. A helpful, familiar team helps alleviate some of that stress. You start feeling more at ease and taken care of when you recognize the same smiling faces every time you come in.

Such trust will lead to patients coming in regularly, which means that problems will be detected and fixed early, thus saving time, money, and pain.

## They Make You Aware of Your Oral Condition and Put You in Charge of It

Proper dentistry does not end when you walk out the door. This is why a powerful dental care team is also conservative. Your hygienist may demonstrate to you the most effective flossing technique, and your assistant may teach you how to look after your teeth following a procedure. The front desk personnel can even assist you with the interpretation of your insurance or payment plans. When you are aware of what happens with your health, you will be more inclined to take care of it in between the visits, and this will produce better outcomes.

## They Provide a Safe, Cozy Atmosphere

An excellent [**dental care team**](/about/meet-the-team-dental/) understands ways of putting the patients at ease. They follow up on you between the treatments, put you through everything that is going on, and respond to your questions without any condemnation. This enables your experience to be more calming, and it also lessens fear, in particular among jittery patients or children.

The proper atmosphere counts. When you have the sense that you can safely and supportively follow through on your dental health, you will tend to do it.

## You Get Rapid, Time-Saving, and Dependable Service

An efficient dental care team understands how to coordinate with each other in minimal time. That implies the reduction of wait time, quicker appointment times, and fewer inaccuracies. Be it rescheduling an appointment or planning a dental procedure, a good team is like a well-oiled machine, and therefore, you will not have a stressful experience. One of the largest factors that keeps patients loyal to a practice is efficiency, and it begins with the team.
